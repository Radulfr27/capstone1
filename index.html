About Me

Hi, my name is Ralph, and I like creating things on the internet. My interest in learning programming started when I was a teenager. I was always fascinated by the video games I played and the tech YouTube channels that I watched back then.

During summer break before entering college, I started learning basic HTML, CSS, and a little bit of Javascript from the website Khan Academy. My aim is to always try to improve the skills that I acquire and to overcome obstacles, even if they're really hard.
